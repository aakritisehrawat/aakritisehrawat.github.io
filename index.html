Encyclia tampensis

Encyclia tampensis var. alba is a type of orchid featured in the Smithsonian Gardens and the United States Botanic Garden's 28th annual orchid exhibition, "The Future of Orchids: Conservation and Collaboration." The exhibition, which runs from January 27, 2024, to April 28, 2024, at the Smithsonian American Art Museum and National Portrait Gallery, showcases around 350 live orchids.

Orchid Specifications:

  • Species: Phalaenopsis Amabilis
  • Height: 45-60 cm
  • Bloom Size: 7-10 cm
  • Color: Pure White

Care Requirements:

  • Light: Bright, indirect sunlight
  • Water: Weekly, moderate
  • Temperature: 18-25°C
  • Humidity: 50-70%

Lycaste virginalis: Bloom

"Lycaste virginalis: Bloom" is the name of one of the orchid blooms included in the Smithsonian Gardens and the United States Botanic Garden's 28th annual orchid exhibition. This exhibition, titled "The Future of Orchids: Conservation and Collaboration," features approximately 350 live orchids and will be on display from January 27 to April 28, 2024.

Orchid Specifications:

  • Color: Pure White

Care Requirements:

Vanda coerulea: Bloom

"Vanda coerulea: Bloom" is another orchid bloom featured in the Smithsonian Gardens and the United States Botanic Garden's 28th annual orchid exhibition, "The Future of Orchids: Conservation and Collaboration." This exhibition, which runs from January 27 to April 28, 2024, will be held in the Kogod Courtyard of the Smithsonian American Art Museum and National Portrait Gallery in Washington, D.C. The exhibition showcases approximately 350 live orchids from the Smithsonian Gardens’ Orchid Collection.

Orchid Specifications:

  • Species: Phalaenopsis Amabilis
  • Height: 45-60 cm
  • Bloom Size: 7-10 cm
  • Color: Pure White

Care Requirements:

  • Light: Bright, indirect sunlight
  • Water: Weekly, moderate

  • Humidity: 50-70%
